Welcome to "Todays Night"

Todays night, is a story game, which your objective is to survive the night, by choosing where to go or what to do with your self.

The Text

When the game start, a line of text will appear, and give you some context about your current situation on the story.

"You open your eyes, it is pitch dark, and you don't remember what happened to you. As you start to regain consioness, you start to fell very cold, and up the hill the is a challet with some lights on, and in the other side of a lake there is a car. what do you do?"

Then the game gives you some options.

Once you select one of the options, the text will change, and the story will continue.

So, are you ready to start the game?
